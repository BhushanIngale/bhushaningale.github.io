London Bike Sharing - Interactive Dashboard

In this project, I have created a dashboard that displays various information such as Wind Speed vs Temperature, and Moving Averages of the count of Bike Rides as per day, week and month. You can easily interact with the dashboard by accessing the dropdowns menus or dragging and holding an area on the timeline.
